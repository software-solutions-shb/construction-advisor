Submit a question

Share your situation and any photos. You will receive independent advisory guidance focused on clarity, options, and next steps. This service is not inspection, certification, approval, or a guarantee of outcomes.

What to include

  • Your name and email
  • Your role (homeowner, contractor, designer, other)
  • A detailed description of the project or issue
  • Multiple photos (if relevant)
  • Urgency indicator and any deadlines

If you are unsure what to provide, describe the decision you are trying to make and include any quotes, specs, or drawings you can share.

Google Form fields (required)

  1. Name (Short answer)
  2. Email (Short answer, email validation)
  3. Role (Multiple choice: Homeowner, Small Contractor, Designer/Installer, Other)
  4. Detailed description (Paragraph)
  5. Photo uploads (File upload, allow multiple files)
  6. Urgency indicator (Multiple choice: Low, Medium, High + deadline date)

Note: Google Forms file uploads require the form owner to be signed in and may require respondents to be signed in, depending on form settings.

Form embed

Replace the placeholder iframe below with your Google Form embed code once the form is created. This is a static site—no backend is required.

Privacy and expectations

Your submission is used solely to prepare advisory guidance. Do not include sensitive information beyond what is necessary to explain your project. This service does not perform on-site assessment or certify compliance.
