This service is designed for decisions that feel "too important to guess." With 30+ years of construction and kitchen experience, the goal is to provide a calm, factual second opinion—especially when timelines are tight and stakes are high.

Guidance is offered in writing, with options and limitations clearly stated so you can move forward confidently with your own team.
