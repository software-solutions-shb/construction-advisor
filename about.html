About and expertise

Independent advisory guidance built on 30+ years in construction and kitchen projects. The focus is on practical decision-making, not sales.

Experience that translates to clear guidance

The advisory service draws on three decades of experience across residential construction, remodels, and kitchen planning. The goal is to help clients evaluate options, identify risks, and make informed decisions with confidence.

The advice is structured, written, and calm—intended for homeowners and small contractors who want a grounded second opinion.

Core focus areas

  • Kitchen layouts, workflow, and storage planning
  • Materials, finishes, and durability trade-offs
  • Scope alignment with quotes and budgets
  • Sequencing, lead times, and project readiness

Advisory scope

Guidance is based on the information you provide and is intended to support your decision-making. This service does not perform inspections, compliance verification, engineering analysis, or permit approval.

When a licensed professional is required, the recommendation will be made clearly.

Values

  • Clarity over complexity
  • Independent, unbiased guidance
  • Respect for budgets and constraints
  • Practical, actionable next steps
