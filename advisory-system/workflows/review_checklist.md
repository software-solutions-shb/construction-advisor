# Human Review Checklist

Use this checklist before approval.

- [ ] Scope is advisory only; no inspection, certification, or guarantees implied.
- [ ] Safety concerns are addressed at a high level.
- [ ] The response is accurate and avoids speculation.
- [ ] Missing information is flagged with targeted follow-up questions.
- [ ] Tone is calm, professional, and non-salesy.
- [ ] Next steps are practical and realistic.
- [ ] Risk level is correctly tagged.
