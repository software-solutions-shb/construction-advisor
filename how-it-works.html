How it works

A simple, structured process focused on clarity and next steps. You provide the context; you receive calm, practical guidance. This is advisory only—no inspection, certification, approval, or guarantees.

1

Submit your question

Use the submission form to describe your situation, attach photos, and note any deadlines. The more context you provide, the better the guidance.

2

Review of materials

Your information is reviewed for clarity, risk signals, and decision points. If something is unclear, you may be asked a follow-up question.

3

Advisory response

You receive a written response with options, trade-offs, and practical next steps. The response aims to help you make a confident decision with your own team.

Boundaries and legal safety

This service provides general advisory guidance based on the information you provide. It does not replace licensed inspections, engineering analysis, permitting, or legal advice. No approvals, certifications, or guarantees are offered.

If your situation requires an on-site assessment, you will be advised to engage a licensed professional in your jurisdiction.

What you can expect

  • Clear explanation of options
  • Identification of common risks
  • Questions to ask your contractor or supplier
  • Practical guidance you can act on

Typical deliverables

  • Written summary in plain language
  • Option comparison and trade-offs
  • Suggested next steps or clarifications
  • Notes on scope alignment and constraints

Response timing

Response timing depends on the complexity of your question and the quality of the information provided. Urgent items are reviewed when possible, but this is not an emergency service.

If time is sensitive, clearly note your deadline in the submission form.
